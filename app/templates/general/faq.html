Frequently Asked Questions

What is LingPen?

LingPen is an academic community platform for linguists and language enthusiasts. It enables sharing of articles, blogs, PDFs, and provides events and courses related to linguistics.

How do I register?

Click "Register" in the navigation bar, fill in your basic information, verify your email, and you're ready to contribute and interact.

Can I upload my own research papers?

Yes! Users can upload PDFs to their personal library under the "Library" section, optionally tagging them for easier discovery.

How do I attend events or courses?

Navigate to the Events or Courses section, find an interesting event or course, and register. For live events, you'll see a countdown and reminders.
