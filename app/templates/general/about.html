About LingPen

LingPen is a community platform designed for linguists and language enthusiasts to connect, share research, publish blogs and posts, attend courses and events, and collaborate academically.

Our mission is to provide a space where people from diverse linguistic backgrounds can engage in meaningful discussions, share research materials (PDFs), attend online and offline events, and learn through structured courses.
